# Governance

### Governance is the Process of Interaction and Decision-Making among the Stakeholders of a Given System or Organization.

MakerDAO governance describes the way rules, norms, and actions are structured, sustained, regulated, and upheld.

#### On-Chain Governance

Maker (MKR) token holders are the primary stakeholders of the system and vote using the Maker Protocol's on-chain governance system.

<CTA>

Learn more about the [MKR token](https://makerdao.com/en/whitepaper/#governance-of-the-maker-protocol)

</CTA>

There are two types of votes in this system, Governance Polls and Executive Votes. Anyone who owns MKR can participate in these votes.
